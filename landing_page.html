Manali

Manali, nestled in the Himalayas, offers a breathtaking getaway with its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and adventure-filled landscapes. Visitors can explore Rohtang Pass, indulge in winter sports, visit the ancient Hadimba Temple, and experience the vibrant local culture. The town's scenic beauty and diverse activities make it a popular destination for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.
